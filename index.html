About Us

What is Shibarium Vault?

Shibarium Vault is an exciting new project that is revolutionizing the cryptocurrency space. It combines the benefits of staking and DeFi to create an entirely unique experience for users. The concept behind Shibarium Vault is to provide users with a secure and efficient way to stake their crypto holdings, as well as a platform for issuing stablecoins pegged to various fiat currencies. With its innovative approach to staking, Shibarium Vault eliminates the need for third-party custodians and presents an open market for lenders, borrowers, and traders alike.

How to buy?

STEP 1

✔️ Download the MetaMask Chrome Extension or mobile app.

✔️ Setup your account & add the Ethereum Chain to your network list.

STEP 2

✔️ Use Binance or another exchange to purchase Eth and send it over to your MetaMask Wallet Address.

STEP 3

✔️ Head on over to Flooz and use the Contract Address to find the Token. Set the slippage tolerance to 6% (but could be higher depending on how much demand there is).
